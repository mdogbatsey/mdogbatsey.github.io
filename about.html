About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Mathematics in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Alabama, working under the supervision of Prof. Shan Zhao and Prof. Yuanzhen Shao.

My research interests span Applied Mathematics, Numerical Analysis, Mathematical Biology, Scientific Computing, and Social Dynamics Modeling — with a focus on developing high-order numerical schemes that bridge rigorous theory and real-world application.
