About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate of Mathematics in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Alabama, working under the supervision of Prof. Shan Zhao and Prof. Yuanzhen Shao.

My research interests include Applied Mathematics, Numerical Analysis, Mathematical Biology, Scientific Computing, and Social Dynamics Modeling.
